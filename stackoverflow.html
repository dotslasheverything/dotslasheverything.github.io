What's it..? How to use it..? Visit the site

What's it..?

Stack overflow is one of the sites in the network of sites, Stack Exchange. It is a Q&A Platform for programmers with around 12 million users from all over the world. You can ask any kind of programming related questions here and someone will answer it. Similarly you can answer other people's questions. Till now around 19 million questions were asked on this platform and around 70% of them got acceptable answers.

How to use it..?

Stack Overflow has some really good things to offer. Not only you can get answers to your coding questions but you can also share knowledge with your coworkers in private and find a job.

Public Q&A

This is the general Stack Overflow.

  • Questions and Answers are open for everyone.But to ask a question or answer a question you need to have an account.

  • However even without an account you can still see other people's questions and answers. You can find the answer with a green tick if its accepted by the user who asked the question. You can upvote or downvote an answer.

  • Explore it at:

    Questions | Stack overflow

Private Q&A: for Teams and Enterprise

This is something of a closed version of Stack Overflow.

  • You can to subscribe for it for your team or for your enterprise.

  • All the features of Public Q&A along with more privacy and security.

  • All the Q&A are open only for people in your team or in your enterprise. They won't be visible in searches or feeds of people out of your team.

  • Explore it at:

    Teams | Stack overflow

Jobs and Advertising

Companies can list their jobs on Stack Overflow. Developers can apply. Explore more at

  • Jobs | Stack overflow
